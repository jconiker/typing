KeyQuest
Learn to type the right way

KeyQuest is a fun, step-by-step way to learn touch typing — typing without looking down at your hands.

  • 🎯Start on the home row, then add new keys, real words, sentences, numbers, and capital letters — like a real typing course.
  • ⌨️The on-screen keyboard lights up the next key and tells you which finger to use, so you learn the correct habits.
  • ⭐Earn up to 3 stars per lesson, beat your best speed (WPM), and watch your accuracy climb.
  • 👨‍👩‍👧Each player has their own progress, so the whole family can practice.
  • 📶Once added to your Home Screen, it works offline — no internet needed.

💡 Tip: rest your pointer fingers on F and J (feel the little bumps) and keep your eyes on the screen, not your hands.
