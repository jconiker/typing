What KeyQuest Does

KeyQuest teaches touch typing — typing without looking down at your hands — in small, friendly steps. Start on the home row, then add new keys, real words, sentences, numbers, and capital letters, just like a real typing course.

The on-screen keyboard lights up the next key and tells you which finger to use, so you build the correct habits from the very first lesson.

Private, Offline & Ad-Free

Once it's on your home screen, KeyQuest works fully offline — no internet needed. Everything runs on your device: no account, no sign-in, and nothing is sent to a server.

And because it's privately developed by Coniker Systems™, there are no ads — ever.

Get KeyQuest on Your iPad

It's a free web app — nothing to buy, no App Store needed. To keep it on your iPad or iPhone so it opens like a normal app and works offline:

  1. In Safari (you're likely here already), tap the Share button — the square with an arrow.
  2. Choose “Add to Home Screen.”
  3. Tap Add — a KeyQuest icon appears on your home screen.
  4. Open it from the home screen. It runs full-screen and works offline.

💡 Tip: rest your pointer fingers on F and J (feel the little bumps) and keep your eyes on the screen, not your hands.

Getting New Features

New lessons and features are added regularly. To get the latest version, just tap 🔄 Update on the home screen while connected to Wi-Fi — it refreshes itself, and your progress is kept. You can check the version number at the bottom of any screen.

Have an idea or found a problem? Tap 💬 Feedback on the home screen to send it straight to us — your feedback shapes what gets built next.
